A human's live-validation report on a real deployment is first-class loop input: triage every finding on the spot — small sharp defects become filed-briefed-fixed-merged within the same session, deeper gaps become tracked enhancements with the live evidence commented onto the existing task. Validation-to-merge distance of one session is the health signal to protect.

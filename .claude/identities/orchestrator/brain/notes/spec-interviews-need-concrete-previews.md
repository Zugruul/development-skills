In spec interviews, every option offered to the human should carry a concrete preview — an ASCII mockup, a config sketch, a flow diagram — not just a label and description. Humans redirect architecture at the option stage when they can SEE the shape (here: a whole bare-brain restructure, engine placement, and observability sinks were decided this way); their notes on a preview map to precise plan edits instead of vague preferences. Related: [[plan-stage-adversarial-review-before-spec]].
